# SRE Workflow Module

## Service Objectives
- Define SLIs and SLO targets.
- Error budget policy.

## Incident Flow
1. Detection and alerting.
2. Initial triage and severity assignment.
3. Mitigation and communication.
4. Recovery and verification.
5. Post-incident review.

## Runbook Design
- Decision tree for first 15 minutes.
- Escalation path.
- Rollback and containment options.

## Reliability Plan
- Top reliability risks.
- Observability gaps.
- Next reliability investments.
