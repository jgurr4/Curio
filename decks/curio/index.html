Vending Machine vs. Supermarket

Curio is the Vending Machine: You tell it exactly what you want. It gives you the item. You leave. No distractions in between.

The Curation Layer

Curio sits above the noise as a terminal-based interface.

  • Browserless: It fetches data, not pixels. No ads can render in a terminal.
  • Spiritually Healthy: AI-driven "Wholesomeness Filters" strip out clickbait and rage-bait.
  • Text-First: Low-stimulation results for high-clarity decisions.

Intent-Driven Discovery

  1. The Ask: discover with specific parameters.
  2. The Filter: Candidate results are scrubbed by hard-coded safety logic.
  3. The Curator: AI analyzes results for truth and relevance, not clicks.
  4. The Delivery: A concise, ranked list with "Trust Signals."

Precision Search

Stop searching. Start finding.

# Find art, not ads
discover music --tags "1950s, bebop, upbeat, high-fidelity"

# Find tools, not "sponsored content"
discover shopping --tags "cast iron skillet, made in USA, lifetime warranty"

# Find truth, not hot-takes
discover research --topic "nuclear fusion progress" --sources "academic, peer-reviewed"

The Guardrails

  • Wholesomeness Tiers: You define the "noise floor" (e.g., G, PG, PG-13).
  • Sandboxed Access: If a browser is needed, it opens a "Single-Use Tab" that expires in 5 minutes.
  • Domain Allow-listing: Only sources the Curator trusts can be opened.
